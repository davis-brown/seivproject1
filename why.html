Why Computer Science?

Why Computer Science?


Well, there are many reasons why you would choose computer science as you major. Computer science is one of the most dynamic and growing areas in the professional world with the continuous growth in digitalization of the world. The following are few of the many reasons computer science is the right field to major in;



1. The Money ($$$)


Computer science is one of the highest paying fields in the world. With computer scientists out of college averaging upwards of $60,000 a year and average salaries being over $85,000 a year, and some making upwards of $130,000 a year, makes computer science one of the most high paying jobs on the planet



2. Job Demand


There is a high demand for computer scientists worldwide, with research showing that the technology industries are short over 1 million skilled computer science globally per year. Just in case you are looking for a major that will secure you a job, computer science is the way to go!



3. Job Satisfaction


Studies have shown that computer scientists tend to have more job Satisfaction than people in many other fields and and that is always a plus when trying to find the right career.



4. Job Opportunities Across Industries


Computer science is such a diverse field that there is a demand for a computer scientist in almost every field in this age and time. They are not limited to only working for tech companies.



5. Critical Thinking and Problem Solving


Computer science requires critical thinking and problem solving skills, it offers a solution to real world problems. If you are looking for a career that will challenge and exercise your critical thinking and problem solving skills, computer science is the major to choose.
